Card Opener for Mingle

A lightweight Chrome extension to call up Mingle cards in the browser.

View project onGitHub

Welcome to the Card Opener for Mingle and Trello (Experimental).

This extension is an effort to make the life of anyone using Mingle or Tello easier. This extension should make it easier to open cards from a cold start as well as while in these applications.

An example:

You receive and email with information related to a card on Mingle. Normally you'd have to open a new tab, navigate to your Mingle project, and then search for the card. Now, all you have to do is open the extension (CMD + [Shortcut of choice]) type in the story number and you're set.
